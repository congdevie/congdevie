COMPANY HISTORY

THE HISTORY OF THIS BUSINESS CAN BE SUMMED UP BY SAYING THAT MY MOTHER ASPIRED TO RUN HER OWN RESTAURANT AS A CHIEF. BACK THEN IN MY MOM DAYS SHE WAS A HIGHLY DILIGENT STUDENT. AFTER THE SCHOOL IN THE MORNING, SHE WORK AS A COOK IN PRIVATE RESTUARANT AT NIGHT. DUE TO A LACK OF FUNDS, HIS ELDER BROTHER NEED FOR FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE FOR HIS MARRINE ENGINERING STUDIES. AS A RESULT, SHE SACRIFICED HER EDUCATION. NOW THAT HER DREAM HAS MATERIALIZED, I WANT THAT ONE DAY GIVE HIM THE RESTAURANT THAT SHE HAD ENVISIONED.
